## Profile Details

### 1. Linda Chen (Product Manager, TechFlow SaaS)
-   **Difficulty**: Medium
-   **Bio**: A hyperactive PM running on cold brew. Squeezed by venture capital targets.
-   **Traits**: Agreeableness: 55/100, Neuroticism: 75/100, Conscientiousness: 40/100.
-   **Red Flags**: Refuses to write down final specs; guilt-trips using verbal agreements; expects weekend support without rush fees.
-   **Quirks**: Starts chats with "Quick question..." or "Just one tiny adjustment!".

### 2. Budi Hartono (Owner, Hartono Culinary Group)
-   **Difficulty**: Hard
-   **Bio**: Traditional food & beverage owner who treats websites as cheap pamphlets. Hates deposits.
-   **Red Flags**: Demands free sample drafts before signing; claims finance holds payments; bypasses tools to WhatsApp call directly.
-   **Quirks**: Ghosts for 3 days and returns late at night demanding quick changes; all-caps texts.

### 3. Sarah Jenkins (Co-Founder, LunarDAO)
-   **Difficulty**: Nightmare
-   **Bio**: VC-backed Web3 founder. Chaotic planning, goldfish attention span.
-   **Red Flags**: Offers utility tokens ($LUNAR) instead of cash; changes layout direction; avoids signing contracts.
-   **Quirks**: Schedules brainstorming sessions at 2 AM; speaks in hype jargon ("LFG", "GM").

### 4. Kevin Cooper (Owner, Cooper & Sons Bakery)
-   **Difficulty**: Easy
-   **Bio**: Elderly baker who wants a simple online sourdough order catalog. Tech-confused but sweet.
-   **Red Flags**: Extremely slow to upload content (confused by cloud drives); consults child family members for UI reviews.
-   **Quirks**: Asks if he needs to buy "internet" separately.

### 5. Evelyn Vance (Head of Brand, GlobalCorp)
-   **Difficulty**: Hard
-   **Bio**: Corporate brand manager with zero risk appetite. Strict guidelines, requires committee sign-offs.
-   **Red Flags**: Iterates revisions without marking milestones; shields herself from blame; demands detailed logs weekly.
-   **Quirks**: Says "Let me align with stakeholders" for simple adjustments.

### 6. Anton Wijaya (Marketing Director, FastRetail Ind)
-   **Difficulty**: Hard
-   **Bio**: High-stress marketing head who treats every task as a code-red retail emergency.
-   **Red Flags**: Asks for direct copying of competitors' pages; gets defensive when scope is set; calls repeatedly.
-   **Quirks**: Bombards single-word messages: "Bro?", "Progress?", "Status?".

### 7. Liam Dubois (Creative Director, Dubois Fashion House)
-   **Difficulty**: Hard
-   **Bio**: Aesthetic perfectionist who rejects standard UX patterns for artistic "soul" and Paris lookbook feel.
-   **Red Flags**: Shifts layout directions on sudden artistic dreams; delays milestone approval for lack of "creative spark".
-   **Quirks**: Poetic feedback: "Make it feel like a rainy Sunday in Paris."

### 8. Maya Sukarno (CTO & Co-Founder, Edutech Indonesia)
-   **Difficulty**: Easy
-   **Bio**: The "Unicorn Client". Highly organized, respects rates and limits, pays exactly on time.
-   **Quirks**: Suggests charging more if specs expand.
